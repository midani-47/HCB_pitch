The Science

Four layers of
molecular insight

HealthBioCare analyses multiple biomarker types simultaneously, giving a complete picture that no single test can provide.

🧬

SNPs

Single nucleotide polymorphisms are inherited genetic variants that shape your unique metabolic fingerprint , your lifelong predispositions to how you process food, respond to stress, and age.

🔵

DNA Methylation

The epigenetic switches that determine which genes are active or silenced. Unlike your DNA sequence, methylation patterns change with lifestyle and are the primary marker of biological age.

🧪

microRNA

Small RNA molecules that regulate gene expression in real time. miRNA patterns from capillary blood reflect your current metabolic state, a window that genetic testing alone cannot open.

📏

Telomeres

The protective caps at chromosome ends that shorten with each cell division. Telomere length is one of the most reliable available indicators of cellular ageing and long-term health risk.
